FAQs
How much can I actually earn? ▼
It depends on your audience size and engagement. A creator with 30k engaged followers mentioning Fiturafy monthly could earn £200-£500/month. Use the calculator above for your estimate.
When do I get paid? ▼
Monthly payouts via bank transfer or PayPal, on the 1st of each month. No minimum threshold — even £1 gets paid out.
Will this affect my existing brand deals? ▼
No. We never interfere with your existing affiliate or sponsorship relationships. Your deals are yours. Fiturafy is additive, not competitive.
What does my creator page look like? ▼
You get a branded page at fiturafy.com/yourname showing all current deals. We're building custom bios and profile photos — coming soon.
Can I leave the programme? ▼
Anytime, no questions asked. Email us and your page comes down within 24 hours. Any pending earnings will still be paid out.
